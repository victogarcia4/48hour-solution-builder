# 48-Hour Website Builder Funnel — Complete Product & Build Specification

## 1. Project Overview

Build a disruptive portfolio/service website for a lean digital studio that offers:

1. Website design
2. Landing pages
3. Ecommerce websites
4. Custom lightweight web applications
5. AI-assisted automations and digital tools

The website must not behave like a traditional agency website. It must function as a **conversion-focused landing page with a guided interactive funnel**. The funnel collects the exact information needed to build a finished website, ecommerce site, or lightweight business platform in less than 48 hours after the client approves the plan and completes the intake.

The entire experience must be:

- Extremely low-friction
- Fast to complete
- Mostly menu-based
- Visually guided
- Mobile-first
- Conversion-focused
- Clear on pricing
- Clear on scope
- Clear on the 48-hour promise

The core business model is based on three disruptive differentiators:


1. **Problem solver** - it is not about building a website/app, it is about solving a small business problem (pain).
2. **Very affordable pricing** — approximately one-half to one-third of what a traditional agency would charge.
3. **Low-friction onboarding** — no long meetings, no complicated forms, no technical language.
4. **Finished product in less than 48 hours** — not a mockup, not a prototype, not an MVP.

## 19. Interactive Funnel Requirements

The funnel is the central feature of the landing page.

### Funnel behavior

- Must be step-by-step.
- Must feel conversational but not chatty.
- Must use mostly buttons, cards, toggles, checkboxes, and dropdown-like choices.
- Every question must include an “Other” option where appropriate.
- When “Other” is selected, show a short text input.
- Questions must dynamically branch based on the selected project type.
- The funnel must show progress.
- The funnel must allow going back.
- The funnel must save answers locally during the session.
- At the end, it must generate a project summary and recommended plan.

### Friction principle

The complexity of the funnel must increase based on project complexity:

| Project Type | Ideal Time | Approx. Number of Questions | Friction Level |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Landing Page | 3–5 minutes | 12–15 | Very low |
| Ecommerce | 5–8 minutes | 18–22 | Medium-low |
| Website + Ecommerce + Web App | 8–12 minutes | 22–30 | Medium but justified |

# 31. Low-Friction UX Requirements

The funnel must be designed to minimize friction.

### Required UX behaviors

- Use buttons/cards instead of long text fields.
- Use multi-select chips where relevant.
- Show one question at a time or grouped micro-steps.
- Include progress indicator.
- Show estimated time to complete.
- Allow back navigation.
- Allow user to skip non-critical questions.
- Use “I am not sure” as an option in relevant questions.
- Use “Other” with a short text field where needed.
- Avoid technical language.
- Use friendly but direct microcopy.
- Show reassurance messages throughout.

Use short messages such as:

> Not sure? Choose the closest option. We can adjust later.

> You do not need perfect content to start.

> Your answers help us build faster.

> This does not lock your final design.

# 35. FAQ Section

Include these questions and answers.

## Why does it cost less than a traditional agency?

Because the process is lean, guided, and technology-assisted. We reduce unnecessary meetings, custom proposal delays, and operational overhead.

## Is the 48-hour website a real finished product?

Yes. For qualifying projects, the result is a finished, functional website, ecommerce site, or lightweight platform within the approved scope. It is not a mockup or MVP.

## When does the 48-hour timeline start?

The 48-hour timeline starts after the client approves the plan, completes the guided funnel, provides required assets or access, and submits the initial payment.

## Do I need to have all the text ready?

No. If you do not have text, we can create base copy using your answers and AI-assisted writing.

## Do I need professional photos?

No. We can work with your available images, stock images, AI-generated visuals, or recommended placeholders.

## Can I request changes?

Yes. Each plan includes up to 2 suggested changes.

## What if my project is more complex?

Complex features, advanced integrations, many products, or multiple user roles may require phased delivery or manual review.

## Do you help with domain and hosting?

Yes. Domain and hosting setup can be included as an add-on or support service.

## Can I pay in parts?

For smaller projects, full payment may be required to start. Larger projects may use a split payment structure.

## What if I do not know what I need?

Choose “I am not sure” in the funnel. The system will recommend a plan or flag your project for review.
